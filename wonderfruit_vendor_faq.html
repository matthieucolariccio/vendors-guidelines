Branding

The no-logo rule

🚫

No logos. Anywhere.

Logos are prohibited on menus, uniforms, tableware, signage, trucks, and all promotional materials — including social media and press unless pre-approved by Wonderfruit Marketing.

🎨 Vibrant hand-painted banners & murals

Prohibited

What you cannot sell

All menu items require prior approval. Our team will review your submission and flag any unsuitable products before the event.

🥥
Beverages Fresh coconuts, packaged coconut water, alcohol, and any beverage sold at Wonderfruit bars.
🚨
Controlled substances Narcotics, cannabis, kratom, or any derivatives. Violations result in immediate booth closure and a fine.
🧴
Plastic — all forms Plastic straws, plastic-wrapped items, single-use cups, and bio-plastics are all banned.

✅ What is allowed

🍃 Compostable containers from paper, wood, cardboard or leaves
♻️ Reusable or refillable cups — attendees bring their own
🥄 Compostable wooden or paper cutlery

Access

RFID wristbands

⌚

Staff Wristbands

Each vendor receives a set number of RFID wristbands for staff. Each band is linked to an individual and cannot be exchanged or resold. Violations may result in fines equal to the gate ticket price.
